Snake Plant

Water when top inch of soil feels dry, water once a week, allow water to drain. Never allow the soil to become saturated.

Tolerates low, medium and bright light. Can grow in any room in your home, or office.

Toxins absorbed by the plant

Trichloroethylene
Formaldehyde
>Benzene
Xylene
